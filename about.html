About Us

Our Story

Alaska Batteries may be a new name in Pakistan's battery market, but the people behind it have spent years living this industry, and in real customer conversations. We started Alaska because we felt the category had become repetitive, the same claims, the same approach, and not enough attention to what customers actually go through when a battery fails at the wrong time.

So we built a customer-first company with a simple belief: power should not be a worry. We focus on understanding how people really use their vehicles and backup systems in Pakistan and then guide them to the right solution, not just a product.

Whether it's choosing the correct battery, improving battery life through better usage and maintenance advice, or standing behind our promise with support, Alaska exists to solve everyday power problems, so our customers can drive, work, and live with confidence.

The Future is Sustainable

To be Pakistan's most trusted power storage solutions brand, enabling uninterrupted mobility, work, and everyday life through dependable performance and responsible innovation. We're committed to building a future where reliable power is not just a service, but a responsibility we uphold with every battery we produce.

The Path to Future

We deliver reliable power solutions designed for Pakistan's real conditions, so customers get consistent performance and dependable backup. Every decision we make, from product development to customer support, is guided by a clear purpose: to be the partner that Pakistanis trust when power matters most.
