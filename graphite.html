Thermal Stability

Graphite additives prevent "Thermal Runaway" during intense Pakistani summers, allowing the battery to operate efficiently even at 55°C ambient temperatures.

02

Charge Acceptance

Because Graphite is highly conductive, it allows for **30% faster charging** cycles, making it ideal for areas with frequent power outages (load shedding).

03

Anti-Sulphation

The graphite carbon layer prevents lead-sulphate crystals from hardening on the plates, effectively doubling the cycle life compared to standard lead-acid.
