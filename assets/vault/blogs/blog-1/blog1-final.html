Check Capacity, Size & Compatibility

Getting the right fit is essential. A battery that doesn't fit or match your load will underperform.

For Vehicles

  • Confirm voltage (12V, 24V, etc.)
  • Verify group size and physical dimensions
  • Check Ah rating for cranking power
  • Confirm terminal layout and polarity
  • Check CCA for winter reliability

For Solar & Storage

  • Calculate daily energy consumption (kWh)
  • Determine desired backup hours
  • Size battery bank correctly (Ah √ó voltage = Wh capacity)
  • Account for depth of discharge (50-80%)
  • Ensure charging system can handle the load

Maintenance & Care

Even premium batteries require proper maintenance. Neglecting care is the top cause of early failure.

  • Clean terminals monthly and remove corrosion
  • Keep terminal connections tight and secure
  • Avoid deep discharges; recharge promptly
  • Test voltage regularly (12.4V+ = healthy)
  • Test weak batteries early to prevent failures
  • Ensure charging system works properly
