Maintenance & Proper Care

Even premium batteries require disciplined maintenance. Neglecting care is the #1 cause of early battery failure.

  • Monthly Terminal Cleaning: Remove corrosion (white/green deposits) with a wire brush
  • Tight Connections: Loose terminals cause voltage drop and starting problems
  • Avoid Deep Discharge: Recharge promptly when depleted‚Äîrepeated deep cycles shorten lifespan
  • Regular Voltage Testing: 12.4V+ = healthy; below 12.0V = needs charging immediately
  • Test Weak Batteries Early: Early detection prevents stranded scenarios
  • Check Charging System: Faulty alternators or chargers undercharge and destroy batteries
  • Keep Cool in Summer: Excessive heat accelerates internal wear‚Äîpark in shade when possible
