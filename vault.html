Answers to your frequently asked questions.

Alaska Batteries are built to last between 2 to 4 years, depending on usage, vehicle type, and environmental conditions. With proper care and regular maintenance, you can maximize performance and extend the battery's lifespan.

Keep terminals clean, avoid frequent short trips, and check voltage regularly. Store batteries in cool, dry places and disconnect them during long-term vehicle storage. Follow our Tips & Techniques section for detailed maintenance advice.

Our warranty covers manufacturing defects, premature failure, and performance issues under normal usage. Physical damage, overcharging, or improper installation are not covered. Always retain your purchase receipt and warranty card.

You can return your old battery to any authorized Alaska Batteries dealer or partner recycling center. We ensure safe, eco-friendly disposal and even offer discounts on new purchases when you recycle with us.

Visit our Find Dealer page or contact our Customer Support to find the nearest authorized Alaska Batteries dealer in your city. Our nationwide network ensures availability and support across Pakistan.

Our batteries come with an extended warranty, depending on the product type. To file a claim, present your original receipt and stamped warranty card at any authorized dealer. After inspection, eligible claims are processed swiftly with repair or replacement.
